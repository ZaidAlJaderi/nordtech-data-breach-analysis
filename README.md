NordTech Solutions – Data Breach Analysis

A structured analysis of a fictional data breach focusing on GDPR, NIS2, incident response, ethics, and organizational responsibilities.

# Project Overview #

This project analyzes a simulated data breach affecting a healthcare-related technology provider, “NordTech Solutions”. The focus is on understanding the legal, ethical, and organizational consequences of a security incident and identifying improvements to policies and processes.

The report covers:

GDPR obligations

NIS2 requirements

Ethical responsibilities

Security and policy failures

Recommendations for improvement

Root-cause and impact analysis

Full analysis is available in the attached PDF.

# Regulatory Focus #
GDPR

The report examines NordTech's obligations regarding:

Lawfulness, fairness & transparency

Data minimization and storage limitation

Security of processing (Article 32)

Breach notification duties (Articles 33–34)

NIS2

Includes analysis of:

Reporting requirements

Business continuity expectations

Organizational responsibilities

Management accountability

# Key Findings #

Lack of strong access management contributed to the breach

Poor logging and monitoring delayed detection

Insufficient internal training created major vulnerabilities

Incomplete incident response procedures worsened the impact

GDPR and NIS2 violations occurred due to slow breach reporting

# Recommendations #

Implement strong identity and access management

Improve logging, detection, and incident response

Strengthen employee training & awareness

Develop clear data retention and deletion policies

Ensure compliance with GDPR & NIS2 reporting timelines
